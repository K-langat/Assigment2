Name Generator

App functionality

This is a web based application which allow people to determine the date which they were born and give a name according to date which the were born reference to Ghana. Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
